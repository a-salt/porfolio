About me

I’m Ai Salt, a designer with a wide skillset and a passion for great sensory experiences. I have been designing and building AV experiences for six years, from world-first theme park rides to immersive exhibitions and smart spaces in the built environment.

Working across industries and cultures has made me adaptable to change and able to handle ambiguity with confidence and positivity. It has also instilled clear communication, an open mind, and the importance of building strong relationships with the people I work with.

As people, our humanness affects the way we interact with digital and physical spaces. So, I approach my work holistically and always strive towards accessibility and inclusion.

I want to produce great solutions and have fun doing it!
