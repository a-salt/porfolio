About me

I’m Ai Salt, a designer with a diverse skillset and a passion for great sensory experiences. I have been designing, building and documenting a wide range of AV solutions for six years, from world-first theme park rides to immersive exhibitions and smart spaces in the built environment.

Working across disciplines, industries and cultures has made me adaptable to change and able to handle ambiguity with confidence and positivity. It has also instilled the importance of clear communication, an open mind, and building strong relationships with the people I work with.

I understand that our humanness affects the way we interact with digital and physical spaces. I approach my work holistically and strive towards diversity, accessibility and inclusion in any project I’m involved in.

Ultimately, I want to produce great solutions with my colleagues and have fun doing it!
